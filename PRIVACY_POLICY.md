# Privacy Policy for BlortNix

**Last Updated: December 8, 2025**

## Introduction

BlortNix ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the BlortNix mobile application.

## Summary

**BlortNix does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share ANY personal information.**

## Data Collection

### What We DON'T Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone, etc.)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Any form of tracking data

### Local Data Storage

The only data stored by BlortNix is:

- **High Score**: Your best score is saved locally on your device using standard device storage.

This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us
- Is not shared with any third parties
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Permissions

BlortNix requires NO special permissions:

- No internet access required
- No storage permissions (uses app-private storage)
- No camera, microphone, or location access
- No contacts or calendar access

## Third-Party Services

BlortNix does NOT use any third-party services including:

- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services

## Children's Privacy

BlortNix is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age, or any user of any age. The app contains no mechanisms for data collection.

## Data Security

Since BlortNix does not collect or transmit any data, there is no personal data at risk. Your high score is stored securely in your device's private app storage.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store app listing page.

## Compliance

This app complies with:

- Google Play Developer Program Policies
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
